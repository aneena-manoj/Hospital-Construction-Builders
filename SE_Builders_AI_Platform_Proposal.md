### Module 4: Construction Safety AI Scanner
**Purpose:** Computer vision system to identify safety hazards automatically

**How It Works:**
1. Upload site photos (from phone or drone)
2. AI scans for safety hazards
3. Generates detailed safety report
4. Assigns priority levels (Critical/Moderate/Minor)
5. Provides OSHA references and remediation steps

**What It Detects:**
- Missing PPE (hard hats, safety vests, etc.)
- Fall hazards (unguarded edges, missing guardrails)
- Electrical hazards (exposed wiring)
- Trip hazards and poor housekeeping
- Equipment safety issues
- Healthcare-specific risks (contamination, infection control)

**Sample Safety Report:**
